omelette Recipe

The easiest way to make the best omelette.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp water
  • pinch salt
  • pinch pepper
  • filling Ingredients

Steps.

  1. Whisk egg,water,salt and pepper
  2. Spray 8-inch non-stick skillet with cooking spray. Heat over ,medium heat. Pour in egg mixture. As eggs set around edge of skillet,with spatula,gently push cooked portions towards centre of skillet. Tilt and push skillet to allow uncooked egg to flow into empty spaces.
  3. When eggs are almost set on surface but still look moist,cover half of omelette with filling. Slip spatula under unfilled side;fold over onto filled half.
  4. Cook for a minute,then slide omelette onto plate.
